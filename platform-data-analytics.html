Get high level microsite visitor data

The great thing about microsites, when compared to the humble PDF or PowerPoint, is that you can collect visitor data.

The real value of data analytics is that it will help you refine your approach as you learn what resonates with your audience.

Granular visitor data will give you a fascinating insight into what your audience cares about the most. Did they watch your video? Did they look through your presentation? How far did they get? You’ll see all this and more.

Easy data compliance

Data is a powerful thing but let’s make sure we keep it compliant.

Zoomforth has built-in features to help you achieve e-Privacy regulations (the so-called cookie law), CCPA, and GDPR data compliance. Your visitors in these regions will be able to manage their privacy preferences, at any time, whilst on your sites.

Get real-time alerts

Want to know exactly when someone is visiting your microsite? Simply set up an alert and we’ll ping you an email to let you know.

This is a really helpful feature if you want to time your follow-up while you are top of mind for your site visitor.

Download or integrate with other data analytics platforms

All your data reports are downloadable as csv files. You can also use JS snippets to pull data into your analytics dashboard e.g. Google, Adobe or Facebook.
